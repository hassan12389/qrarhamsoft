How It Works

Custom QR Code with Logo

Set QR Content

Select a content type at the left for your QR code (URL, Text, Email...) After selecting your type you will see all available options.

Customize Design

Set a color & replace the required shape of your QR code. The corner & body can be customized individually. Add logo into your QR code.

Generate QR Code

Set the pixel resolution of your QR code with the slider. Click the "Create QR Code" button to see your qr code preview.

Download Image

Every thing perfectly done. Now you can download the image files for your QR code as .png or .svg, .pdf, .eps vector graphic.
